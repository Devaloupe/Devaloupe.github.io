Integrity CRC checker

Description: Given plaintext, normalize text to be all lowercase letters and convert them and print them as integers and hexadecimals. Then, convert them to binary and print the binary. Pad the characters according to given CRC types. Then, perform a CRC algorithm and print the result. Finally, print the final message in hexadecimal.

I learned how to work with binary strings, convert characters and integers to binary and vice versa, and implement a simple integrity checker in C code.
